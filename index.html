About

I am an experimental high-energy nuclear physicist with extensive experience in detector R&D, data acquisition, and Monte Carlo simulations. My work spans Gas Electron Multiplier (GEM), RPC, Cherenkov, straw tube, and scintillator detectors, along with GEANT4-based simulation, detector calibration, and DAQ/analysis for precision electroweak measurements in the MOLLER experiment at Jefferson Lab.

Research Interests

  • Detector R&D: GEM, RPC, Cherenkov, straw-tube, scintillators
  • Monte Carlo simulation (GEANT4), reconstruction & performance studies
  • DAQ systems, signal processing, calibration & stability
  • Electroweak precision tests; di-muon physics in high-rate environments
