About

I am an experimental high-energy nuclear physicist specializing in detector R&D, data acquisition systems, and precision electroweak measurements. My work focuses on Cherenkov detectors for the MOLLER experiment at Jefferson Lab and future physics opportunities at the Electron-Ion Collider (EIC).

Current Research

My current research focuses on precision electroweak measurements in the MOLLER experiment, including Cherenkov detector optimization, photon yield studies, and high-rate DAQ systems. I am also exploring near-threshold J/ψ production and nucleon structure studies at the EIC.
