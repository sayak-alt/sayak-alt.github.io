Research Highlights

Research directions and scientific highlights

This page gives visitors a focused overview of the work you want them to remember first: detector development, precision measurements, and future research opportunities.

MOLLER

Cherenkov detector characterization and precision instrumentation

Current work includes characterization and optimization of Cherenkov detectors for the MOLLER experiment, photon-yield studies, cosmic-ray test stand development, and waveform analysis workflows for detector response.

Detector R&D

GEM-based tracking, readout systems, and performance studies

Prior and ongoing work includes GEM detector performance evaluation, high-rate detector studies, readout integration, stability and efficiency characterization, and calibration-driven analysis.

Simulation

Monte Carlo and performance optimization

GEANT4-based studies, detector-response modeling, acceptance considerations, and signal interpretation for experimental systems.

Data acquisition

DAQ and waveform workflows

Readout systems, signal processing, electronics integration, calibration, and software support for streaming and event-based analysis modes.

Future programs

EIC-related interests

Near-threshold J/ψ studies, detector feasibility work, and structure-function opportunities connected to the Electron-Ion Collider.
